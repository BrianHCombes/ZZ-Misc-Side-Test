NOTE: Have discovered you can only object fit one dimension (say width for example) while holding height unchanged when you use the "cover" attribute. With this you can then crop the sides away.
NOTE: If you do modify the other dimension the object-fit property will resize the proportionality to fit that other dimension. In other words the object-fit:cover attribute will fit the image to whichever dimension the image can fill. You can then modify the other dimension for cropping purposes.
